## 2025-02-14 - Fix Insecure Randomness in Particle Physics
**Vulnerability:** Use of predictable `Math.random()` in `src/physics/Particle.ts` for generating particle properties like size, velocity spread, and debris scatter.
**Learning:** Security-critical systems or general physics simulations that aim for unpredictability or fairness should not rely on `Math.random()`, which is a pseudo-random number generator (PRNG) and not cryptographically secure. While its impact in pure visual particles might be considered low, standardizing on the project's provided `secureRandom()` ensures consistency and defense-in-depth across the codebase.
**Prevention:** Use `secureRandom()` from `src/utils/Security.ts` or `MathUtils.secureRandom()` uniformly across the codebase instead of `Math.random()`. Configure linting rules to flag `Math.random()` usage where applicable.
